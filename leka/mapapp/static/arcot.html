ARCOT

Arcot is famous for its unique style of biriyani, known as Arcot biriyani. This flavorful dish is made with fragrant rice, tender meat (usually chicken or mutton), and a blend of aromatic spices, creating a distinctive taste that is cherished by food enthusiasts.Apart from Arcot biriyani, the town is known for its culinary delights and traditional dishes that reflect the region's culinary heritage. Local specialties and delicacies attract food lovers from different parts of Tamil Nadu and beyond.
