RANIPETTAI

Ranipet district is one of the 38 districts in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It was formed in April 2020, bifurcating from the Vellore district. Ranipet district is located in the northern part of Tamil Nadu, and its administrative headquarters is in the town of Ranipet. Ranipet district is known for its industrial significance. It houses several industrial complexes, including the Ranipet Industrial Complex, which is one of the largest industrial areas in Tamil Nadu. The district has a strong presence of industries ranging from chemical manufacturing to leather processing.
