WALAJAPET

Walajapet has historical significance due to its association with the Battle of Wandiwash (also known as the Battle of Vandavasi) fought between the British East India Company and the French East India Company in 1760. The British victory in this battle was a turning point in the Carnatic Wars, leading to British dominance in South India .Like many towns in the region, Walajapet has an economy driven by agriculture, trade, and small-scale industries. Industries such as leather processing, textiles, and manufacturing contribute to the local economy.
